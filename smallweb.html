The small web

Sometimes I long for an internet that is more personal and boring and less dopamine optimzied. So here I am just going to leave some links to some places on the small internet that I think are nice.

Jeremy Maluf has a post he updates every couple of years with the contents of a single backpack with which he travels the world. Not featured is the fact that you need to build, sell a company and be excessively rich to have this lifestyle work for you.

Etherium founder Vitalik has a similar blog where he as a less dignified version. I find it not to be a coincidence that the factor that facilitates these lifestyles are more the extreme wealth than the small number of items in a bag.
