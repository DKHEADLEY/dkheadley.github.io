2023

Rory Stuart—Politics on the Edge

A not-so-optimistic-review of what is wrong with British politics. Rory has the property of somehow being right on every issue despite being a raging royalist. Worth reading.

Peter Zeihan—The Accidental Superpower: The Next Generation of American Preeminence and the Coming Global Disorder

Too depressing a book for non-American readers.

2022

Peter Zeihan—The End of the World Is Just the Beginning: Mapping the Collapse of Globalization

A prescient book that goes, somewhat hyperbolically, some way to help you understand how the world order is faltering from the status quo of the last almost century. Strongly recommended.
All of Zeihan's work would benefit from more citations and some critical takes from others.
